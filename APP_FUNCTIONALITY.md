# Research Africa Application Functionality

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the platform's features, grouped by user type.

## User Types & Functionalities

### 1. Platform Super Admin

-   Onboard new journals and configure global platform settings
-   Manage all journals, users, and roles across the platform
-   Access platform-wide analytics and reports
-   Assign or remove Journal Admins
-   Manage system-wide notifications and templates
-   Troubleshoot and support all journals

### 2. Journal Admin

-   Create and configure their journal (details, branding, editorial board)
-   Add/edit/remove journal users (Editors, Reviewers, Authors, Contributors)
-   Assign roles and permissions within their journal
-   Oversee all submissions, peer review, and editorial workflow for their journal
-   Access journal-specific analytics and reports
-   Communicate with editorial board and contributors

### 3. Editor

-   Manage article submissions for their journal
-   Assign reviewers to manuscripts
-   Track review progress and send reminders
-   Make editorial decisions (accept, revise, reject)
-   Communicate with authors and reviewers
-   Manage editorial board assignments

### 4. Reviewer

-   View and accept/decline assigned manuscripts
-   Download manuscripts and supporting files
-   Submit structured peer review feedback and recommendations
-   Communicate with editors (if enabled)
-   Track review history and deadlines

### 5. Author

-   Submit new manuscripts to journals
-   Track status of their submissions (under review, revision, accepted, rejected)
-   Respond to editorial and reviewer feedback
-   Revise and resubmit manuscripts as needed
-   View decision letters and publication status

### 6. Contributor

-   May include guest editors, section editors, or other custom roles
-   Permissions vary by journal (e.g., limited editorial or review access)

### 7. Publisher

    -   Access articles that are ready for publication
    -   Upload galley proof (PDF only) for author approval
    -   Trigger email notification to corresponding author for galley proof approval
    -   View galley proof approval status (pending, approved, rejected)
    -   Upload final ready-to-publish version after author approval (PDF only)
    -   Approve or reject final publication of articles
    -   Manage publication schedule and release
    -   Communicate with editors and admins regarding publication status

## Core Platform Features

    -   **Journal Onboarding:** Guided process for adding new journals, setting up editorial boards, and configuring workflows
    -   **User & Role Management:** Assign users to journals with specific roles; manage permissions and access
    -   **Article Submission & Management:** Authors submit manuscripts; editors manage workflow; all actions tracked
    -   **Peer Review Workflow:** Multi-stage review, reviewer assignment, feedback, and editorial decisions
    -   **Publisher Galley Proof Workflow:** Publisher uploads galley proof (PDF), corresponding author receives approval link, author can approve/reject, publisher uploads final version after approval
    -   **Editorial Board Management:** Add/invite board members, manage roles and terms
    -   **Notifications & Communication:** Automated emails for all workflow events; in-platform messaging
    -   **Analytics & Reporting:** Submission stats, review turnaround, editorial decisions, and more
    -   **Security & Access:** Secure authentication, password management, and role-based access control
