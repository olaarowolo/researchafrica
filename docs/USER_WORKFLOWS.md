# Research Africa User Workflows (Architectural Diagrams)

## 1. Platform Super Admin Workflow

```
[Super Admin]
    |
    v
[Onboard Journal] ---> [Configure Global Settings]
    |                          |
    v                          v
[Assign Journal Admins]   [Manage Users/Roles]
    |                          |
    v                          v
[Monitor Analytics] <--- [Support & Troubleshoot]
```

---

## 2. Journal Admin Workflow

```
[Journal Admin]
    |
    v
[Configure Journal]
    |
    v
[Add/Edit Users] ---> [Assign Roles]
    |                      |
    v                      v
[Oversee Submissions] --> [Monitor Analytics]
```

---

## 3. Editor Workflow

```
[Editor]
    |
    v
[Manage Submissions]
    |
    v
[Assign Reviewers]
    |
    v
[Track Reviews]
    |
    v
[Make Editorial Decisions]
    |
    v
[Communicate with Authors/Reviewers]
```

---

## 4. Reviewer Workflow

```
[Reviewer]
    |
    v
[View Assigned Manuscripts]
    |
    v
[Download Manuscript]
    |
    v
[Submit Review]
    |
    v
[Track Review History]
```

---

## 5. Author Workflow

```
[Author]
    |
    v
[Submit Manuscript]
    |
    v
[Track Submission Status]
    |
    v
[Respond to Feedback]
    |
    v
[Revise/Resubmit]
    |
    v
[View Decision/Publication]
```

---

## 6. Contributor Workflow

```
[Contributor]
    |
    v
[Access Journal (as assigned)]
    |
    v
[Perform Editorial/Review Tasks]
```

---

## 7. Publisher Workflow (with Galley Proof)

```
[Publisher]
    |
    v
[Access Ready Articles]
    |
    v
[Upload Galley Proof (PDF)]
    |
    v
[System: Notify Corresponding Author]
    |
    v
[Author: Approve/Reject Galley Proof]
    |
    v
[If Approved] ---> [Upload Final Version (PDF)]
    |
    v
[Approve/Reject Final Publication]
    |
    v
[Manage Publication Schedule]
```
