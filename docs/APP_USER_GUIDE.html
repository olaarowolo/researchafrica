Research Africa Application: User Guide & Workflows

This page explains how the Research Africa platform works, how to use it, and provides step-by-step workflow diagrams and explanations for each user type.

How the App Works

  • Journals are onboarded by Super Admins and managed by Journal Admins.
  • Users are assigned roles (Admin, Editor, Reviewer, Author, Contributor, Publisher) with specific permissions.
  • Manuscripts are submitted by Authors, reviewed by Reviewers, managed by Editors, and published by Publishers.
  • Notifications and workflow status updates are sent automatically at each stage.
  • All actions are tracked for transparency and analytics.

How to Use the App

  1. Register or be invited to a journal as a user with a specific role.
  2. Log in to access your dashboard and see your tasks and permissions.
  3. Follow your workflow (see below) to submit, review, edit, or publish articles.
  4. Check notifications for updates, feedback, and required actions.
  5. Admins can manage users, roles, and journal settings from their dashboard.

User Workflows & Explanations

1. Platform Super Admin

Explanation: The Super Admin manages the entire platform, onboarding new journals, configuring global settings, assigning Journal Admins, managing all users, and monitoring analytics. They provide support and troubleshooting for all journals.

7. Publisher (with Galley Proof Workflow)

[Publisher]
    |
    v
[Access Ready Articles]
    |
    v
[Upload Galley Proof (PDF)]
    |
    v
[System: Notify Corresponding Author]
    |
    v
[Author: Approve/Reject Galley Proof]
    |
    v
[If Approved] ---> [Upload Final Version (PDF)]
    |
    v
[Approve/Reject Final Publication]
    |
    v
[Manage Publication Schedule]
Explanation: The Publisher accesses articles ready for publication, uploads a galley proof (PDF), which triggers an email to the corresponding author for approval. The author can approve or reject the galley proof. If approved, the Publisher uploads the final version (PDF) and manages the publication schedule.
